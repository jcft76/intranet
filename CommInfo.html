Relay for Life

Relay for life are organising an event this year at Tabcorp park on the 24th February 2018. 
Relay For Life is a fun and moving overnight experience that raises funds for Cancer Council’s vital work in cancer research, prevention, information and support. It’s also a chance for the Melton community to recognise and celebrate local cancer survivors, patients and carers, and to honour and remember loved ones lost to cancer.
Join the thousands of Australians who take part in Relay For Life each year in the fight against cancer. Start or join a team in the Melton 2018 Relay today!

for more information visit their website : https://www.relayforlife.org.au/
